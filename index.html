About Ecommerce in Uganda



Ecommerce in Uganda is rapidly growing with numerous platforms emerging to cater to the increasing demand for online shopping. Major players in the market include Jumia and Jiji, which offer a wide range of products from electronics to fashion. The growth of mobile money and improved internet connectivity have significantly contributed to the rise of ecommerce in the country.

Despite challenges such as logistical issues and limited internet penetration in rural areas, ecommerce continues to expand, providing opportunities for entrepreneurs and consumers alike.
